About Bunni

The Birthplace of Coffee

Ethiopia is where coffee was discovered, and Bunni carries forward this proud legacy to the world.

Rooted in Ethiopian Heritage

Bunni General Business PLC was founded with a mission to share Ethiopia's finest coffee with the world. Based in Addis Ababa, we work directly with farming communities across Ethiopia's most celebrated coffee-growing regions.

Our name "Bunni" comes from the Amharic word for coffee — a testament to our deep connection to Ethiopia's coffee heritage and culture.

  • 🌍 Global Export Network — Shipping premium beans to 30+ countries across Europe, Asia, North America, and the Middle East.
  • 🤝 Direct Trade Relationships — Working hand-in-hand with Ethiopian farmers, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices.
  • ✅ Quality Certified — ECX certified, meeting international standards for specialty and commercial grade coffee.
  • 🏭 Full Processing Facilities — From cherry to green bean, we handle washing, drying, hulling, and grading in-house.

Our Services

End-to-end coffee solutions from farm to your facility.

🚢

Coffee Export

We export premium Ethiopian green coffee beans to roasters, distributors, and importers worldwide. Bulk and specialty lots available year-round.

🏪

Bulk Distribution in Ethiopia

Large-scale domestic distribution of roasted and green coffee across Ethiopia. We supply supermarkets, hotels, restaurants, cafes, and wholesale buyers with bulk orders from 50kg bags to full container loads.

🔬

Quality Control

Rigorous cupping and grading at every stage. Our Q-graders ensure only the highest quality beans carry the Bunni name.

📦

Retail & Custom Packaging

Available in 250g, 500g, and 1kg retail bags plus 50kg bulk sacks. Private label and custom branding available for distributors and retailers worldwide.

🌱

Sustainable Sourcing

Direct partnerships with farming cooperatives, supporting sustainable agriculture, fair wages, and community development programs.

📋

Trade Consulting

Expert guidance on Ethiopian coffee trade regulations, ECX procedures, logistics, and market intelligence for international buyers.

How We Work

From Farm to Export

Our meticulous process ensures every bean meets the highest standards.

1

Sourcing

Direct partnerships with farming communities across Yirgacheffe, Sidamo, Harrar, Guji, and Kaffa regions.

2

Processing
